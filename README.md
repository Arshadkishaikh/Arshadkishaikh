### 🥈 2. India CPI Inflation Analysis (2013–2023) — Economic Analytics

> **The most unique project in the portfolio — real government data, economic domain depth**

**Tools:** `Microsoft Excel` `Pivot Tables` `Statistical Analysis` `Data Visualization`

Analysed **10 years of official government CPI data** (124 monthly observations, 27 product categories, 3 sectors) to understand how inflation in India changed across demonetisation, GST rollout, COVID-19, and the Russia-Ukraine commodity shock.

**Key Findings:**
- 📈 India's prices rose **+71.2% cumulatively** over 10 years (General CPI: 104.6 → 179.1)
- 🍛 Food & Beverages (nearly 50% of the basket) consistently outpaced headline inflation — meaning households felt *more* price pain than official numbers showed
- 🛢️ Meat & Fish, Oils & Fats, and Transport move almost in lockstep with global crude oil prices (r > 0.85) — rural households consistently paid above the national average

**What it demonstrates:** Excel mastery · Statistical correlation · Economic domain knowledge · 5 structured problem statements · PDF report writing

### 🥉 3. Ecommerce Sales Dashboard — Power BI with PostgreSQL

> **Shows SQL as a live data source + DAX time intelligence — a core mid-level analyst skill**

**Tools:** `Power BI` `PostgreSQL` `DAX` `Power Query`

Built an interactive sales dashboard connected directly to a PostgreSQL database, displaying **YTD Sales ($2.06M), Profit ($233.68K), Quantity (18.9K units), and Profit Margin (11.35%)** — with DAX time-intelligence measures automatically comparing current year vs. prior year.

- 🎛️ Segment-level slicers (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office) let non-technical managers explore data with one click

**What it demonstrates:** Live SQL data connection · DAX time intelligence (TOTALYTD, SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR) · YoY variance analysis · Dashboard UX design
